Frequently Asked Questions

Is HermesVPN free to use? +
HermesVPN offers both free and premium plans. The free version includes basic features with limited bandwidth, while premium plans provide unlimited bandwidth and access to all servers.
How secure is the VPN connection? +
We use AES-256 encryption, the same standard used by governments and security experts worldwide. Your data is completely protected from hackers and third parties.
What device can I use HermesVPN on? +
HermesVPN is available on Windows and macOS platforms. We are working on expanding support to mobile devices and other operating systems in the near future.
Will VPN slow down my internet? +
While encryption adds minimal overhead, our optimized servers ensure you experience minimal speed reduction. Most users don't notice any significant difference in browsing speed.
What if I need help or support? +
Our support team is available 24/7 via email and live chat. We also have comprehensive documentation and video tutorials to help you get started.
